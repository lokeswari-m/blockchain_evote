Core Features

Built for Trust & Transparency

Every component is engineered to ensure election integrity, from cryptographic hashing to database-level security.

Immutable Blockchain

Every vote is mined into a proof-of-work block with SHA-256 hashing. Once recorded, votes cannot be altered, deleted, or tampered with.

Double-Vote Prevention

Database-enforced UNIQUE constraints guarantee each voter can only cast one ballot. Duplicate attempts are automatically rejected at every layer.

Vote Verification

Voters receive a unique blockchain hash as a digital receipt. Verify your vote's existence and integrity on the chain at any time.

Real-Time Results

Election results update instantly as votes are cast. Monitor live turnout percentages, candidate standings, and chain statistics.

Role-Based Security

Separate admin and voter interfaces with Supabase Auth. Row-Level Security policies enforce data isolation at the database level.

High Performance

Built with Flask and optimized queries. Vote submission under 500ms, results under 100ms, and block mining in 1-3 seconds.

How It Works

Three simple steps to a verifiable, blockchain-secured vote.

1

Authenticate

Voters log in with their unique ID. Admins authenticate via secure Supabase credentials with role verification.

2

Select & Submit

Browse candidates, make your choice, and confirm. The system verifies your eligibility before proceeding.

3

Blockchain Sealed

Your vote is mined into a new block with proof-of-work. You receive a SHA-256 hash as your tamper-proof receipt.
